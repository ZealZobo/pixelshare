About PixelShare

We're building the most creator-friendly image platform on the internet. No compression, no watermarks, no limits — just your vision, shared beautifully.

Our Mission

PixelShare was founded in 2024 with a simple belief: everyone deserves a beautiful place to share their visual stories. Whether you're a professional photographer, a hobbyist, or someone who just loves taking pictures, we've built a platform that puts your work first.

We don't compress your images into oblivion. We don't slap watermarks on your art. We don't sell your data. We just provide the best possible canvas for your creativity.

Creator First

Every decision we make starts with one question: "Is this good for creators?" If the answer is no, we don't do it.

Privacy Matters

Your images are yours. We use end-to-end encryption and never train AI models on your content without explicit consent.

Blazing Fast

Our global CDN ensures your images load in milliseconds, no matter where your audience is located.

Truly Unlimited

No arbitrary storage limits. No bandwidth caps. Share as much as you want, as often as you want.
